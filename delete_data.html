Request Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from our app, you can do so in two ways:

  • 1. In-app Deletion:
    Go to: Settings → Delete → Confirm deletion.
    Your account and data will be removed from our servers.
  • 2. Request via Email:
    Send an email to: nadexaisolutions@gmail.com
    Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
    Include your registered email in the request.
Data Retention:
Once your account is deleted, all associated personal data is removed permanently. Some data may be retained for legal or analytical purposes (non-personal).
Personal Information:
We may collect basic user details such as your name, email address, and user ID for authentication and app personalization purposes. We do not collect sensitive personal information like address, phone number, ethnicity, or beliefs.
File Access Notice:
This app may generate and save PDF documents locally on your device for your personal use. No files or documents are uploaded, collected, or stored on any external server.
Financial Information:
We collect financial-related information such as transaction amounts, supplier payments, fuel history, and sales records entered by users via text fields. This data is not connected to any actual payment method, gateway, or credit scoring system.
