Data & Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account or specific types of data from our app, we offer two easy options for your convenience:

1. In-App Deletion

You can delete your data directly within the app using the Delete button available for each data type in list view. The following data categories are individually deletable:

  • Employee History
  • Supplier History
  • Transaction History
  • Account Data

2. Request via Email

If you prefer, you may request data or account deletion by sending an email:

Email: nadeemflutter@gmail.com
Subject: Data Deletion Request

Please mention your registered email or user ID in the email for verification.

Data Retention

Once your data or account is deleted, all personal information will be permanently removed from our systems. Some anonymous or analytical data may be retained for legal or reporting purposes.
